About Site

StickShift UI

As a moderator on the /r/stickshift subreddit, I constantly see curious individuals asking questions about manual transmissions. There are a ton of helpful communities out there answering questions and discussing pros, cons and intricacies of driving a manual transmission.

One thing that's always felt missing was a central place with some information. Not a forum or a website where you have to register, log in and sign away your soul just to gain entry. Just a webpage that has useful information you can access quickly.

I've tried to write this website 3 times (this is the 4th iteration!), and I've never been able to accurately design it to convey the right information in the right way.

I hope anyone who finds this site useful shares it with their friends and it becomes a useful tool for anyone looking to learn some interesting facts about their car!
